Instructions

This game is designed to help you get better at mental arithmetic. It has two main sections:

  • The Main Game Field
  • The Revision Field

To Play the Game:

  1. Enter your name to begin.
  2. Choose the category you would like to practice.
  3. You have 30secs to answer as many questions as you can.
  4. Each right answer will be logged above the question field.
  5. Each wrong answer will be logged to the revision field.
  6. After the 30secs, you will have time to revise the questions you got wrong.

    7. Happy learning!
